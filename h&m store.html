H AND M

Hennes & Mauritz AB or H&M Group (abbreviated and stylized as H&M) is a multinational clothing company based in Sweden that focuses on fast-fashion clothing for anyone, any gender. As of 23 June, 2022, H&M Group operated in 75 geographical markets with 4,801 stores under the various company brands, with 107,375[b] full-time equivalent positions.[7] H&M is the second largest international clothing retailer.[8] H&M was founded by Erling Persson, and the shop's current CEO is Helena Helme

The company was founded by Erling Persson in 1947 when he opened his first shop in Västerås, Sweden.[10] The shop, called Hennes (Swedish for "hers"), exclusively sold women's clothing.

H&M sources raw materials from different areas around the world. The top three locations that ship its raw materials are China, Bangladesh, and India. Its retail headquarter is located in Sweden, where 21 suppliers and factories manufacture H&M's clothing products and accessories.
